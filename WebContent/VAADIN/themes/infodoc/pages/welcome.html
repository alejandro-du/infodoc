InfoDoc Pro offers you an easy to use web application to execute and control business processes such as:

  • Ticket and Incident Management
  • Mail Management (Requests and Responses)
  • Service Management
  • Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
  • Task Management
  • Project and Requirement Management
  • Invoice Management
  • Document Management

With InfoDoc Pro you can create forms (cases, tickets, requests, responses, services, customers, tasks, activities, projects, requirements, and invoices management) with custom properties (fields) that will be managed executing activities. An activity allows you to manipulate or modify the properties associated to a form.

First steps

1. Create groups and users
Select "Group" and "Users" options in the "Admin" menu and create the groups and users you need.
2. Create forms to manage your processes data
Select the "Forms" option in the "Configuration" menu and define your forms.
3. Put properties into your forms
Select "Properties" in the "Configuration" menu and define all the properties requiered to fill out your forms.
4. Create activities
Select the "Activities" option in the "Configuration" menu and define the steps for your processes.
5. Explore the documentation and video tutorials
Read the available documentation in the "Help" menu and explore the video-tutorials seccion of this page.

Video Tutorials

How to configure InfoDoc Pro as a Ticket/Incident Management System?

A Ticket is a customer service case that pass through states such as opened, in progress, and closed. Each Ticket has information (properties) about the customer and the service case itself.

How to configure InfoDoc Pro as a Mail Management System (Requests and Responses)?

A request is a document that arrives at a company. A response is a document that leaves the company. A Mail Management System manages the workflow of the activities with this documents.

How to configure InfoDoc Pro as a Customer Relationship Management System (CRM)?

A simple CRM solution must address the management of customers, appointments, and telephone calls. A customer has contact and personal information (properties). Also, a list of appointments and telephone calls is needed per customer.

How to configure InfoDoc Pro as a Project Management System?

A project has properties such as name, customer, participants, requirements document, risks, next delivery dates, milestones, and requirements.
